Chief Creative Officer  
Publicis Groupe | Japan

Erick was born and raised in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. 

Rio, Boston, New York, Lisbon, São Paulo, Singapore and now Tokyo (almost in that order) are the cities he’s had the luck to call home.

His career as a creative has spanned roles with Leo Burnett in Portugal, and with JWT, FCB and Havas in Brazil. And before joining Publicis Groupe as CCO in Japan, he was the Executive Creative Director at Mullen Lowe in Singapore for four years, where he also led the Unilever Oral care brand ‘Close-Up’ globally.

Throughout his career Erick has had the privilege to work with some amazing, creative people– and was very fortunate to be part of teams that won several awards, several times— in every country and region he’s worked.

These include Cannes, D&AD, The One Show, Spikes Asia, London International, Clio, Art Directors, El Ojo, El Sol (Spain), CCP (Portugal), CCSP (Brazil) and Agency of the Year for two consecutive years in Singapore (CCA).

He always carries his trusted pocket camera with him and loves documenting his daily routine with pictures.

Some of them can be seen on the links above– including his photography website and instagram.

And more recently, he put together two editions titled ‘notes to future francisco’, and ‘ notes to future nina’ which consist of hundreds of observations from ‘present day erick’— to the grown up version of his kids.
